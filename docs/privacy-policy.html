CatcherCom — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 24, 2026

CatcherCom is a dugout pitch-calling app. It does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. The app works entirely on your device and has no servers, accounts, analytics, or advertising.

Microphone

CatcherCom uses the microphone only for the live "push-to-talk" feature, so your voice can play through a paired Bluetooth earpiece in real time. Audio is never recorded, saved, or sent anywhere — it is passed through locally only while you are holding the talk button, and it stops the moment you release it.

Data we store

Your settings (pitch list, voice choice, and preferences) are saved only on your device. We do not have access to them, and they are never uploaded.

No network use

CatcherCom does not connect to the internet, use third-party services, or include any tracking or analytics SDKs.

Children's privacy

Because CatcherCom collects no data from anyone, it collects no data from children. The app is designed for coaches and contains no accounts, messaging, or external links.
